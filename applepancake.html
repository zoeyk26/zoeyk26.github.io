Mima's Apple Pancake

Serves Cooking time Preheat oven to
6
40 minutes
400 fahrenheit

Ingredients

  • Apples (3-4 Granny Smith's, sliced thin)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, eggs, sugar, vanilla, and salt
  2. Melt 3 tablespoons butter in pyrex baking dish
  3. Add apples to pyrex baking dish
  4. Pour egg mixture over apples
  5. Bake in 400 degree oven for about 10 minutes or until top browns
  6. Top with our creative sugar mixture
Recipe can be doubled and then baked longer
