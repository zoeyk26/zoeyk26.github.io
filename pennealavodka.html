Jen's Penne Ala Vodka

At a Glance

Serves

4

Cooking time

20 minutes

Preheat stove to

Medium heat

Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups (8 oz) Penne, uncooked
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh garlic
  • 1 3/4 (14 1/2 oz can) diced tomatoes, drained with liquid reserved
  • 1 teaspoon diced basil leaves
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese
  • 3 tablespoons of vodka
    • all seasonings can be "to taste"

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to directions on box, drain and toss with olive oil
  2. While water is boiling, you can begin making the sauce. In a medium skillet over medium heat melt the butter and add the garlic (don't let it brown), tomatoes with 1/4 cup of the reserved tomato liquid, basil, salt, and pepper
  3. Heat until boiling
  4. Reduce heat and simmer until most of liquid is reduced (5 minutes)
  5. Add heavy cream and vodka, heat on low and add cheese
  6. Heat until thickened
  7. Toss pasta and sauce
