Sherry's Casserole Barbeque Chicken


At a Glance

Serves

6

Cooking time

1 3/4 hours

Preheat oven to

350°F

Ingredients

  • 3 to 3 1/2 of ready to cook broiler (whole chicken), cut up
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 cup salad oil or fat
  • 1 sliced medium onion
  • 1/2 cup celery, chopped
  • 1/4 cup minced green pepper
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons worcestshire
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

  1. Early in day, dip chicken pieces into flour mixed with salt
  2. Fry in hot oil until golden brown
  3. Remove chicken to 3 quarter casserole
  4. If necessary, pour all but 2 tablespoons oil from skillet
  5. Add onion to oil, saute till golden and tender
  6. Add celery and next 6 ingredients (green pepper, ketchup, water, worcestshire, brown sugar, pepper)
  7. Pour over chicken
  8. Chill
  9. About 2 hours before serving heat oven
  10. Bake chicken covered 80 minutes then check
  11. Cook 25 minutes longer, or until tender
    12. *Total cooking 1 3/4 hours
