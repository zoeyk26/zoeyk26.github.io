The Berk's Chicken with Mushrooms

At a Glance

Serves

4

Cooking time

30 minutes

Preheat oven to

350°F

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast strips (2lbs chicken)
  • Garlic-salt
  • Onion powder
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Sauteed garlic
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 cans of chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breast strips with garlic-salt, onion powder, pepper and flour
  2. Add chicken stripes to pan with small amount of olive oil and sauted garlic
  3. Saute chicken for about 7 minutes per side
  4. Place chicken in a baking pan
  5. Add sliced mushrooms to garlic and oil mixture and saute mushrooms about 10 minutes - until brown
  6. Add 2 cans of chicken broth and about 1/4 cup parmesan cheese to mushrooms in pan
  7. Allow to simmer for a few minutes
  8. Pour over chicken and bake - yummy and light!
