Sue's Fiesta Cheese Spread

At a Glance

Serves

6-8

Cooking time

10 minutes
(active)

Preheat oven to

N/A

Ingredients

  • 1 package (8oz) cream cheese softened (can be fat free)
  • 1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 1 can (4oz) green chilis undrained
  • 2 teaspoons chopped pimentos
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped green onions with tops

Ingredients

  1. In medium bowl, combine cream cheese and salt
  2. Beat until light and fluffy
  3. Fold in chilis, pimento and onion
  4. Chill covered for 2-3 hours
  5. Set stand at room temperature for 15 minutes before serving
    6. *Serve with crackers
