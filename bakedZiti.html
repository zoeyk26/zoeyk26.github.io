Nana's Baked Ziti

At a Glance

Serves

6-8

Cooking time

1 hour

Preheat oven to

350°F

Ingredients

  • 1 box Ziti
  • 1 pound long chopped meat
  • 1/2 pound cheese (mozzerela)
  • 1 small low fat ricotta
  • 1 egg
  • 1 jar tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Brown meat place and plate with paper towel to drain fat
  2. Mix ricotta cheese and egg in pan from meat with sauce and mozzerela cheese
  3. Boil ziti
  4. Cook and drain and add ziti to pan
  5. Place in casserole dish and bake 20 minutes

*Take this recipe with a grain of salt
