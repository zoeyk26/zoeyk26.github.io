Anu and Serena's Applesauce Cake

Preheat oven to
350 fahrenheit

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon club soda
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup apple juice
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup applesauce
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients and bake for about 40 minutes to an hour or until toothpick comes out clean (use loaf pan or bundt pan)
  2. Enjoy!
