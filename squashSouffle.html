Mima's Squash Souffle

At a Glance

Serves

Side dish: 8-10
Main dish: 6-8

Cooking time

1 hour

Preheat oven to

350°F

Ingredients

  • 2 packages Frozen Squash (southland) - defrost before using
  • 16 oz coffee rich
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 stick melted butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix squash to wet ingredients (coffee rich, eggs, and butter)
  2. Mix dry ingredients
  3. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients
  4. Top with cinnamon and bake at 350°F for 1 hour and 15 minutes

*Can be made the day before and baked for approximately 15 minutes. Regrigerate and then finish cooking the next day for about an hour
