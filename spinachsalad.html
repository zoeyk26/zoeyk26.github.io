Andrea's Spinach Salad with Strawberries

At a Glance

Serves

6-8

Cooking time

15-20 minutes

Preheat oven to

N/A

Salad Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs spinach (remove stems)
  • 1 1/2 pints strawberries (cut in pieces)

Dressing Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon grated onion
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Salad Instructions

  1. Toss spinach and strawberries in medium bowl
  2. Mix salad with dressing mixture

  1. In blender combine sugar and vinegar
