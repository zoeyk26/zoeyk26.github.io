Nana's Brisket

At a Glance

Serves

4

Cooking time

3 hours 45 minutes

Preheat oven to

First 90 mins: 325°F
Last 2 hrs 15 mins: 250°F

Ingredients

  • 5 lb brisket
  • 3/4 cup + 12oz water of frozen pink lemonade
  • 1 jar Heinz chili sauce
  • 1 pack Lipton onion soup mix powder
  • 3/4 cup box light brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix lemonade, chili sauce, onion soup mix, and brown sugar and pour over and around meat before cooking. Also, surround the meat with baby carrots, baby potatos, and sweet potatoes in its tinfoil tub
  2. Cook in glass covered tight with tinfoil. IMPORTANT - Cook fat side up. Get point, not flat cut brisket
  3. Take out, tent with tinfoil for 15 minutes
  4. Carve
