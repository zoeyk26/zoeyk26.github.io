Michelle's Blueberry Pancakes

At a Glance

Serves

Approximately 16
4-inch pancakes

Cooking time

Fast!

Heat stove to

Medium heat

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon - juice from 1 lemon
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups unbleached all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and cooled slightly
  • 1-2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Whisk lemon juice and milk in medium bowl and set aside
  2. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt
  3. Whisk egg and melted butter into milk until combined
  4. Make well in center of dry ingredients in bowl, pour in milk mixture and whisk gently until just combined (a few lumps should remain)
  5. Heat non-stick skillet on medium heat for 3-5 minutes
  6. Brush skillet with oil
  7. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon blueberries on each pancake
  8. Flip when bubbles appear
  9. Serve immediately
