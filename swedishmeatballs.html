Nana's Sweet and Sour Meatballs

At a Glance

Serves

6-8

Cooking time

2 hours

Preheat oven to

350°F

Meatball Ingredients

  • 1 lb meat (turkey and veal) ground
  • 4 slices of Pettridge Farms cinnamon raisin bread
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Sauce Ingredients

  • 2 cans Hunt's tomato sauce
  • 2 jars baby food applesauce
  • 2 jars baby food apricot
  • Lemon juice to taste
  • 1/4 box brown sugar (dark)

Meatball Instructions

  1. Mix into small balls
  2. Bake in oven on a rack for about 20 minutes for fat to drip off
  3. Cook sauce

Sauce Instructions

  1. Stir and mix under low heat for 10 minutes
  2. Add meatballs
  3. Cook for remaining time
