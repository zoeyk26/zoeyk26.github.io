Marissa's Peanut Butter Rice Krispies Bars

At a Glance

Serves

2 dozen
bars

Cooking time

10 minutes

Preheat oven to

Just a microwave!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon butter or stick margerine
  • 6 cups rice krispies
  • Cooking spray
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter chips

Instructions

  1. Combine the peanut butter and butter in a large microwave safe bown. Microwave high for around 45 seconds or until melted.
  2. Add marshmallows and microwave for around 1 1/2 minutes, stirring every 30 seconds
  3. Add cereal to peanut butter mix and toss until mixed
  4. Pat cereal mixture into a 3x9 baking pan coated with cooking spray
  5. Place peanut butter chips in small microwave safe bowl and microwave for around 30 seconds or until melted
  6. Spoon melted chips in a heavy duty ziplock bag; seal. Snip a tiny hole in 1 corner of bag; drizzle melted chips over cereal mix
  7. Cool slightly; cut into 24 pieces
