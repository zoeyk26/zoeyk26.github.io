Jen's Baked Beans

At a Glance

Serves

8

Cooking time

2 1/2 hours

Preheat oven to

350°F

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb slab bacon
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 (1 lb) bag large lima beans
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspooon dry mustard
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Cook beans in 6 cups of water with bacon for 1 1/2 hours
  2. Drain and save juice
  3. Saute 1 onion and 1 clove of garlic in 2 tablespoons of oliv oil
  4. Add 1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder, tomato soup, all juice, Worcestershire sauce, dry mustard, brown sugar, vinegar and salt
  5. Pour beans into a large baking pan
  6. Cover with above mixture
  7. Bake at 350 fahrenheit for 35 to 45 minutes, covered, and 15 minutes, uncovered
