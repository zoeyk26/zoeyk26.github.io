Donna's Pumpkin Bread

At a Glance

Serves

Loaves

Cooking time

1 hour

Preheat oven to

350°F

Ingredients

  • 3 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup salad oil
  • 2 cups raisins
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 15oz can pumpkin or 1 3/4 cup
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup walnuts or pecans
  • 2/3 cup water

Instructions

  1. Combine sugar and liquids
  2. Add dry ingredients and mix well
  3. Pour into greased loaf pans and bake
  4. Cool for about 20 minutes and then turn onto wire tacks
