Debra's Slaw


At a Glance

Serves

15

Cooking time

30 minutes

Preheat oven to

N/A

Slaw Ingredients

  • 1 lb. cole slaw (bag)
  • 2 bags Ramen noodles beef flavor (reserve flavor packets for dressing)
  • 1 cup pecans
  • 1 cup sunflower seeds
  • 2 bunches scallions

Dressing Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup mazola oil
  • 2/3 cup white wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Ramen noodle beef flavoring

Instructions

  1. Empty slaw package
  2. Crumble ramen noodles and pecans
  3. Add sunflower seeds and scallions
  4. Mix dressing separately
  5. Put dressing on right before serving (otherwise it will get soggy)
    6. *Triple the recipe if making for 60 or more
