Nana's Hamburgers

At a Glance

Serves

4

Cooking time

10-15 minutes

Preheat oven to

325°F (broil)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 package Lipton onion soup mix
  • 2-4 tablespoons BBQ sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 bread crumbs
  • 4 slices of cheese (American?)
  • 4 buns

Instructions

  1. Heat oven
  2. Shape meat and place in oven
  3. Broil meat
  4. Turn after 7-10 minutes
  5. Take out of oven, place on bun and cheese and serve
