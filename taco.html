Allyson's Burritos/Tacos

At a Glance

Serves

4

Cooking time

45ish minutes

Preheat stove to

Medium/High heat

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs grount turkey meat
  • 4 ripe avocados
  • 1 box of tomatoes
  • 1 bag of shredded mexican cheese
  • 1 jar of mild salsa
  • 10ish tortillas
  • 2 cups water???
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning???
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cover the non-stick pan with olive oil and turn the heat up to medium-high
  2. When the olive oil is hot, add all of the turkey, stirring occasionally, until it is all browned (there should be no pink)
  3. Add water and taco seasoning, and stir until the water has dissipated
  4. Transfer meat to serving dish
  5. Meanwhile, slice the avocado and tomato into small cubes and place in bowls along with cheese
  6. Enjoy! Zoey makes them the best:)
