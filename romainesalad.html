Sharon's Strawberry/Romaine Salad

Serves
6-8

Ingredients

  • 1 large head romaine lettuce
  • 1 medium purple onion
  • 1 pint strawberries
  • 1/2 cup mayo or miracle whip
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons poppy seeds

Instructions

  1. Tear lettuce into bite size pieces
  2. Slice onion thin
  3. Slice strawberries
  4. Mix together mayo, vinegar, sugar, milk, and poppy seeds
  5. Mix well and add to lettuce, onions, and strawberries
  6. Toss thoroughly and ENJOY - Delicious SUMMER salad!!
