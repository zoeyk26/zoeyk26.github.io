Mima's Sweet Potato Pie

At a Glance

Serves

2 pies

Cooking time

20 minutes

Preheat oven to

400°F

Ingredients

  • 3 large cans yams
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • Maple syrup
  • 1/2 stick melted butter
  • Orange juice

Instructions

  1. Drain juice from canned yams
  2. Put yams in large mixing bowl
  3. Add all ingredients (honey, salt, syrup, butter, orange juice) together in a large bowl and beat with electric mixer until smooth and almost all lumps are gone
  4. Taste mixture and add more of above as needed
  5. Transfer to glass pie plates
  6. Dot each pie with dots of butter
  7. Bake for 10 minutes
  8. Dot with many, many, marshmallows (but not too close to plate's edge)
  9. Bake for another 10 or 15 minutes until marshamallows are brown
  10. Enjoy! (especially the mostly marshmallow pie the next day as we have always loved to do!)
