Mima's Apple Pancake (Sugar Mixture)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Top pancake with sugar mixture and dot with butter
  2. Bake about 10-15 minutes longer
