Jocelyn's White-Chocolate Cranberry Cake

At a Glance

Serves

16

Cooking time

1 hr 25 mins

Preheat oven to

350°F

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 package (9 squares) Baker's Premium White Baking Chocolate divided
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) butter, softened
  • 1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons grated orange peel
  • 5 tablespoons orange juice, divided
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons Calumet Baking Powder
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Grease and flour 10-inch tube pan or fluted tube pan
  3. Chop 6 of the chocolate squares; set aside
  4. Beat butter and sugars in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy
  5. Add eggs, orange peel and 3 tablespoons of the orange juice
  6. Mix well
  7. Add flour and baking powder; beat until well blended
  8. Stir in chopped chocolate and the cranberries
  9. Bake for 1 hour - 1 hour 10 minutes
  10. Cool in pan 10 minutes
  11. Remove from pan to wire rack; cool completely
  12. Place remaining 3 chocolate squares and remaining 2 tablespoons orange juice in small sauce pan
  13. Cook on low heat until chocolate is completely melted, stirring frequently
  14. Drizzle over cake
  15. Let stand until glaze is firm
  16. ENJOY! XOXO! Yummy:)
