Anu and Serena's Black Bean Dip

At a Glance

Serves

6-8

Cooking time

40 minutes

Preheat oven to

350°F

Ingredients

  • 1 can vegetarian refried beans
  • 1 can black beans drained
  • 1 can mild/medium tomatoes and chilis (or use salsa)
  • 1 pint fat free sour cream
  • 1 bag mexican cheese

Instructions

  1. In a casserole, mix together beans
  2. Top with tomatoes, then sour cream, then ceese
  3. Bake covered for 20 minutes and uncovered for 20 minutes
  4. ENJOY!
